# exemplars/

Drop 1–3 **human-written** samples here (`.md` or `.txt`) to calibrate the LLM
judge to a specific voice — your own, or whoever you're writing as. The judge uses
them for *style*, not content.

These files are git-ignored on purpose: your writing samples stay local and never
get committed or sent anywhere except, at judge time, to the model you call. The
deterministic grader and the detector don't use this folder at all.
